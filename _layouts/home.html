Want to understand Data-Centric AI better?

Data-Centric AI is the approach to AI development that considers the training dataset as the centerpiece of the solution instead of the model.

Coined by Andrew Ng, Data-Centric AI has become a movement.

The Data-Centric AI Community is the place to discuss data quality for data science.
